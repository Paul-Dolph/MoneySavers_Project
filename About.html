The Challenge:

Millions around the world struggle with budgeting and saving their money. In some situations, this leads to poverty or the unfortunate situation of debt. To combat this, we have created a “money-saving” website. Our site helps users save money, keep track of their personal finances, and puts it all in an easy to see format.
